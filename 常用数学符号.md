## demo

- 多行公式

  $$
  \begin{align}
  D(x) &= \int_{x_0}^x P(x^{\prime})\,\mathrm{dx^{\prime}}  \\
  &= C\int_{x_0}^x x^{\prime n}\,\mathrm{dx^{\prime}} \\
  &= \frac{C}{n+1}(x^{n+1}-x_0^{n+1}) \\
  &\equiv  y
  \end{align}
  $$
